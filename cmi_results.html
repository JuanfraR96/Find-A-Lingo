Welcome to the NBCMI Interpreter Registry. This listing is made available to the public and anyone seeking an NBCMI credentialed medical interpreter. You can search the directory by language, state, certification type, name, or CMI or Hub-CMI number.

All active interpreters are automatically included on the NBCMI Interpreter registry. If you do not wish to continue being listed, please let us know and we will gladly remove your name from the listing.

There are some SCAM emails being sent to NBCMI members. The writers claim to have a job offer from companies called Pharma Match, Abbvie, Custom Pharma Services, Biogen, Fresenius Medical Care, Pharma Group or Scinopharm. Please ignore these emails! You may also visit the following link for more information on scams to be aware of:

https://nbcmi.memberclicks.net/assets/docs/Updated_Registry_and_Cyberscam_Alert.pdf

Please be cautious and report any potential scammer to your local authorities and the Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3): www.ic3.gov.
